# Marc Lou's 32 Principles of Viral Products

This skill guides the agent in applying indie hacker Marc Lou's rules of viral product creation, copywriting, pricing, and conversion optimization.

---

## 🎨 1. Landing Page UI & Colors
- **Three Colors Only**: Maintain a strict palette. Black text, white background, and exactly **one accent color** for the primary CTA/Buy button to prevent attention dilution.
- **Show before Explaining**: Put an interactive demo or a clear screenshot/video of the dashboard above the fold in the Hero section. Let users see the interface before reading paragraphs of copy.
- **One Idea per Screen**: Frame the landing page sections clearly. Each scroll section must focus on one single concept (e.g. just the problem, just the demo, just the comparison, just the pricing).

---

## ✍️ 2. High-Converting Copywriting
- **Numbers over Adjectives**: Replace weak adjectives like "rápido", "fácil", or "barato" with concrete statistics (e.g., instead of "controle rápido de gastos", use "organize suas contas em 5 minutos por semana").
- **Fifth-Grade Headline**: Keep the hero headline extremely simple. If a 10-year-old or your mom cannot immediately explain what the product does, rewrite it.
- **Emotional Hook & Desires**: Sell outcome, status, time, or money savings, not technical features (e.g., instead of "sincronização de tabelas em tempo real", use "pare de brigar por dinheiro com o seu parceiro").
- **Strong Assertive Claims**: Avoid weak, defensive words like "talvez", "geralmente", or "quase". Make bold statements, not estimates.

---

## 💰 3. Pricing & Call-to-Actions (CTAs)
- **One Call to Action**: Have exactly one primary path on the screen. Multiple CTAs lead to hesitation and analysis paralysis.
- **Action-Oriented CTAs**: Replace generic labels like "Enviar", "Começar", or "Cadastrar" with the actual next event (e.g., "Começar a Economizar", "Visualizar Meu Dashboard").
- **Popcorn Pricing & One-time Payments**:
  - Keep pricing tiers to 3 choices at most: Good, Better, Best.
  - Prioritize one-time payments (lifetime access) over subscriptions. It is 10x easier to sell and converts significantly higher.
- **Hard Paywalls**: Validate demand early. If nobody is willing to pull out their card, you don't have validation.

---

## 📣 4. Viral Elements & Comparatives
- **Competitor Comparison**: Include a direct, simple table comparing your product's strengths against legacy spreadsheets or bank apps.
- **Shareable Footer**: Place a memorable quote, referral link, or founder's message at the very bottom.
